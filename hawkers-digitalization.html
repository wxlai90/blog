Why like that

lwx 29 May 2021 on Coding, Covid19

I came across this article today while having my morning coffee about how a portion of hawkers in SG is being left behind in the digitalization race accelerated by Covid-19.

Article

Today Online

While I have always regarded a certain level of obsolescence as a necessary evil in the transition to a more digital society. I couldn’t help but wonder if this was more evil than necessary.

I narrowed down the obstacles faced by the hawkers to 2 main factors:

  1. They are not digitally savvy enough to register for food delivery platforms like grab food, food panda, etc. Which also it tough for them to start selling online.

  2. The fees charged by the food delivery platforms are off-putting and makes it less worthwhile to even contemplate the idea.

That’s when I realized, there might be something I can do to help.

I have always had a similar idea for digitalizing hawkers and food centres. I had previously worked on my personal project of achieving such a platform, but stopped when I couldn’t find time.

I am thinking, maybe it’s time to revive the idea and write a new platform.

However, this would be different from the existing ones in a few ways:

  1. There will be no fees.
  2. Delivery service is provided by volunteers/ad-hoc workers.
  3. Option to request for volunteers to help onboard is possible.

Having no fees should not be a big problem unless it grows super fast and super big. Which would be a happy problem anyway.

Delivery is a brokering concept, where people can offer their service whenever they want and an asking price. It is then up to the stall owners to accept it. If they don’t feel it’s worthwhile, they will have to reject the order or figure out some other delivery fallback service.

An option to request for volunteers to help them onboard. There are way too many people who can easily navigate a website and register a stall. Especially younger people like teenagers, maybe I am too optimistic but I feel like all we need to do is let them know who needs help and people will help.

And so, I start to code my new project “Hawkers SG”.
