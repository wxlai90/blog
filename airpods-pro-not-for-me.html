Airpods Pro Not For Me

lwx 28 Nov 2021 on Airpods, Apple

My AirPods Pro

So I got my AirPods Pro back in Sept 2021. There was an online store sale by Courts, and I snapped them up for just $264.00. Which was kinda a steal.

There were a few hiccups though.

  1. The warranty coverage has started in July, 2 months earlier than my actual purchase date.

  2. There was a blue hairline plastic thread embedded deep into the charging case.

When I noticed the blue thread, I was worried that it could be a counterfeit. But Courts did have a good enough reputation. With the two issues I faced, I decided it was worth a trip down to the Apple Store in Orchard.

When I was there, the staff told me there was nothing he can do for the warranty, and I had to call a separate hotline - which I did and got it sorted out on the same day.

As for the blue thread, he brought it to the service engineer whom used a pair of tweezers to pull them out. Or so he claimed. I didn’t check thoroughly but on the following day, I actually saw the blue thread again. I believe what happened was the thread got lodged in the hinge and nobody noticed it. Knowing what they would do (tweezer it), I decided to just yank it out myself. I tugged hard on it and heard a snap sound. Pretty sure part of the thread is still lodged inside the bottom part of the case (but whatever).

I don’t like it

Apart from the poor out-of-box experience. I realize the silicon tips hurt my ears. Unlike my 1st gen airpods, which I can listen to all day and still don’t feel any discomfort.

The spatial audio is a gimmick that I don’t really like either.

The only good feature is the ANC, but even then there are much better ANC earbuds.

The case’s battery drains at a constant rate even when I don’t use them at all. Which is kinda weird.

Overall, I don’t like the Airpods pro for their poor fit to my ears. And since Airpods 3rd Gen just launched, perhaps I will get a set for myself this Christmas.
